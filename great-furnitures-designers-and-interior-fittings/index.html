About Our Furniture Studio

Founded in 1976, 3 Great Furnitures Designers and Interior Fittings has been a trusted name in Nairobi's furniture manufacturing industry for over four decades.

Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create furniture that stands the test of time.

Located in Kasarani Constituency, we've earned a 4.9-star rating for our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.
