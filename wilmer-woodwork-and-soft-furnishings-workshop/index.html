About Our Workshop

Wilmer Woodwork And Soft Furnishings Workshop has been serving Nairobi with high-quality custom furniture since 1995. Our skilled craftsmen combine traditional techniques with modern designs to create pieces that last generations.

Our Story

Founded by master carpenter Wilmer Ng'ang'a, our workshop started as a small family business on Ngong Road. Over the years, we've grown into one of Nairobi's most trusted furniture makers, known for our attention to detail and commitment to quality.

We source only the finest materials, including sustainably harvested hardwoods and premium fabrics, ensuring our products stand the test of time.
