Our Story

Located in the heart of Embakasi, Tangerine Furniture has grown from a small showroom to one of Nairobi's most trusted furniture stores. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us a 4.4-star rating from our valued customers.

We specialize in both ready-made and custom furniture solutions, ensuring that every home can find pieces that perfectly match their style and needs.
