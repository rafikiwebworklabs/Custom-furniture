About Impala Furniture

Kenyan Craftsmanship Since 2010

Located in the heart of Nairobi at Lenana B, Riruta, Impala Furniture has been creating beautiful, durable furniture pieces for homes and businesses across Kenya.

Our team of skilled artisans combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern design to create furniture that stands the test of time.
